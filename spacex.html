History

Key milestones

2002

Founded

Elon Musk founds SpaceX with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling Mars colonization.

2008

Falcon 1 reaches orbit

After three failures, Falcon 1 becomes the first privately developed liquid-fueled rocket to reach Earth orbit.

2015

First booster landing

Falcon 9 lands its first stage back on a pad for the first time — reusable rockets become real.

2020

Crew Dragon flies

SpaceX sends NASA astronauts to the ISS, becoming the first private company to do so.

2023+

Starship era begins

Integrated flight tests of the world's largest rocket push the boundaries of what's possible.
